Riposte Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-03

Riposte is a Chrome extension that suggests AI-drafted replies for LinkedIn posts and comments, generated using your own Claude subscription.

What Riposte does with your data

  • When you click into a LinkedIn comment box, Riposte reads the text of the post (and the specific comment you're replying to, if applicable) from the page you're already viewing.
  • That text is sent to a small companion application that you install and run on your own computer (see riposte-releases).
  • The companion app sends that text to Anthropic's Claude, authenticated with your own personal Claude subscription login, to generate a reply suggestion.
  • The suggestion is returned to the extension and shown to you. You choose whether to insert it, edit it, or ignore it. Riposte never posts anything on your behalf.

What never leaves your computer

Nothing you read or write through Riposte is sent to any server operated by the developer of this extension. The companion app runs entirely on your own machine; the only third party involved is Anthropic (via your own Claude account), which you already have a separate relationship and agreement with.

What's stored, and where

  • A one-time pairing code is stored in your browser's local extension storage (chrome.storage.local) so you don't have to re-enter it. This never leaves your browser.
  • The companion app stores its own pairing code and a rotating log file on your own computer, under %LOCALAPPDATA%\Riposte. Nothing here is transmitted anywhere.

Data sale and sharing

Riposte does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. There is no analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind in this extension.
